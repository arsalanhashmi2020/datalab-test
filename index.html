Welcome to DataLab

DataLab is your comprehensive platform for managing economic data. We offer robust tools for data storage, analysis, and visualization. Whether you're tracking inflation trends or comparing economic variables across regions, DataLab helps you gain meaningful insights efficiently.

  • Insert: Add new economic datasets with relevant metadata.
  • Visualize: Generate graphs and visualizations for analysis.
  • Read: Access previously uploaded datasets for research.
  • Update: Modify existing dataset information as needed.
  • Delete: Remove outdated datasets securely.
